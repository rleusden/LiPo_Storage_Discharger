## Storage Discharge Test

The graph below shows a discharge test performed with the LiPo Storage Discharger prototype.
Invalid measurements caused by meter range changes, disconnected probes and logging interruptions were removed from the dataset.
The circuit successfully discharged the cell to approximately 3.82V, which is within the recommended LiPo storage voltage range of 3.80V–3.85V.
